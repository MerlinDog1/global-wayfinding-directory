## Notes

- **Endpoint**: UK-headquartered with strong Singapore presence. Landmark project: DUO towers Singapore. Pan-regional capability.
- **Corlette Design**: Australia HQ'd but operates globally. Luxury hospitality specialist. Has Singapore presence.
- **Mijksenaar**: Dutch heritage with Hong Kong presence. Strategy-first. Global airport/transport portfolio.

### Tier 2 - Regional Anchors

**Singapore:**
- **Mazefate**: Singapore HQ consultancy with strategic wayfinding focus. Retail, hospitality, mixed-use strength.
- **UDS Design**: Established 1993. Full-service sign consultancy with fabrication. Long track record in Singapore.
- **Radical Design**: Since 2010. EGD expertise with code compliance specialism.

**Hong Kong:**
- **Signix**: Established consultancy serving China, HK, Asia. Wide sector coverage.
- **graphiaBrands**: Pioneer of EGD in HK/China. Strong placemaking focus.
- **CCD/Cheng Chung Design**: Premium hotel/residential specialist. ATG BEYOND division for signage.
- **Marc & Chantal**: Architecture-integrated wayfinding. Sheung Wan HQ.

**Japan:**
- **MOTIVE Inc.**: Tokyo wayfinding studio. Notable rail/transport work.
- **Kenya Hara**: World-renowned designer. Award-winning hospital signage. High design prestige.
- **Nippon Design Center**: Major Japanese design firm with signage division.

**Australia:**
- **Micio**: Melbourne HQ. Full-service strategy to design. Multi-sector coverage.
- **Studio Semaphore**: Award-winning. Airport credentials (Perth, Melbourne). ARUP partner.
- **Maynard Design**: International firm with Melbourne/Sydney presence. Transport/urban realm specialist.
- **Vivid Wayfinding**: Multi-city presence (Gold Coast, Brisbane, Melbourne). Award-winning.

### Key Observations
1. **Singapore is the regional hub** - Most regional consultancies HQ'd or operating from Singapore
2. **Hong Kong has strong local specialists** - Multiple EGD pioneers based in HK
3. **Japan has design prestige** - World-renowned designers (Kenya Hara) but fewer pure wayfinding firms
4. **Australia has mature market** - Multiple established specialists across Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Perth
5. **Cross-border capability exists** - Endpoint, Corlette, Maynard operate across regions

### Gaps Identified
- **Singapore**: Limited gap - good consultancy coverage
- **Hong Kong**: Strong coverage - multiple quality specialists
- **Japan**: Limited pure-play consultancies - mostly design agencies with signage work, or large manufacturers
- **Australia**: Strong coverage - multiple quality specialists
