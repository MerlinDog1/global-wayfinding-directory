- **Modulex**: Global brand with São Paulo office. Full-service EGD across all sectors.

- **CLA Programação Visual**: Market leader in Brazilian signage/wayfinding.

### Tier 3 - Strong Regional (Chile)
- **Wayfinding Consultores**: Only pure-play wayfinding consultancy in Chile. Strong credentials.

### Tier 4 - Regional/National (Colombia)
- **Nazca Señaletica**: Airport credentials (El Dorado).

### Tier 5 - Emerging/Limited (Argentina, Peru, Panama, Costa Rica)
- **Martin Alba**: Argentine wayfinding specialist.
- Limited pure-play wayfinding firms in Peru, Panama, Costa Rica - served by regional/international players.

## Notes

### Key Observations
1. **Brazil is the LATAM anchor** - Largest market with multiple established players (Modulex, CLA, Placrim, STUDIOMDA)
2. **Chile has a strong pure-play specialist** - Wayfinding Consultores is the only dedicated wayfinding consultancy
3. **Colombia has airport credentials** - Nazca worked on El Dorado, major regional airport
4. **Peru, Panama, Costa Rica underserved** - Limited local pure-play wayfinding firms; served by US/global firms
5. **Central America covered by Panavision** - Regional player covering 7 countries

### Gaps Identified
- **Argentina**: Need more verification of current status for listed firms
- **Peru**: Very limited local wayfinding specialists found
- **Panama**: No pure-play Panama wayfinding firms identified
- **Costa Rica**: No pure-play Costa Rica wayfinding firms (RSM Design serves from US)

### International Firms with LATAM Presence
- Modulex: Denmark HQ, Brazil office
- RSM Design: USA HQ, Mexico + Costa Rica projects
- Steer: UK origin, Bogotá office
