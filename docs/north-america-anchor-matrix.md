## Notes

### Key Observations
1. **USA dominates the region** - Most premium strategy-led EGD firms are headquartered in the US
2. **RSM Design is the regional anchor** - Has presence in all three NA countries (US HQ, Canada office, Mexico projects)
3. **Canada has strong local presence** - EDG, Modulex, Cygnus, EEC all provide full-service wayfinding
4. **Mexico emerging** - Urbanetica leads local wayfinding; US firms (RSM) serve luxury hospitality there
5. **Cross-border capability varies** - US firms mostly domestic; only RSM, SPD, Mijksenaar have true cross-border NA + international

### Gaps Identified
- **Mexico**: Limited pure-Mexican wayfinding consultancies. Need more local firms identified.
- **Canada**: Need verification of current status for listed firms
- **USA**: Some regions underserved (e.g., Southeast, Southwest)
