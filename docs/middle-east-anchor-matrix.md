## Notes

### Tier 1 - God-Tier / Premium Consultancy
- **LIMAH**: Pioneer of modern wayfinding in Middle East. Landmark projects: Al Qana (Abu Dhabi), Diyar Al Muharraq (Bahrain), La Mer (Dubai), Bulgari Resort, Yas Bay, Deira Mall. Wayfinding + customer experience + public art.
- **Creative Dialog**: "200+ projects for leading developers." Full BIM integration. Strategy-forward consultancy with end-to-end delivery. Known for dual Arabic-English design expertise.
- **GVC (Green Vision Consultants)**: Regional leaders with explicit cross-border coverage (UAE, KSA, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain). BIM LOD 300. Sustainability-focused. Mega-developments focus.

### Tier 2 - Strong Premium
- **GLSW**: Dubai-based, worldwide operations. Focus on "comprehensive and engaging experience journeys." Notable for master-planned destinations.
- **Dezign Technic**: 21 years experience. Offices in UAE, Qatar, Lebanon, Egypt, UK, Spain. Full "concept to closeout" service. Notable project: Infinity des Lumières (Dubai).
- **Projective**: KSA-headquartered with UAE presence. 10+ years UAE experience. Strategy + fabrication.
- **Dar Al-Handasah**: Large regional AEC firm with dedicated EGD team. Pan-Middle East presence.

### Key Observations
1. **UAE is the regional hub** - Most premium consultancies are headquartered in Dubai
2. **Cross-border capability is key** - Most anchor players operate in 3+ GCC countries
3. **Strategy-forward firms command premium** - Those offering full "concept to closeout" or BIM integration are differentiated
4. **KSA expansion is accelerating** - With Vision 2030 mega-projects, KSA-based firms (like Projective) are growing rapidly. New delivery partners emerging (PHC, SignEx, ProSigns) to support implementation
5. **Bilingual Arabic-English expertise is critical** - Differentiator for regional vs international firms
6. **KSA market has limited native strategy consultancies** - Most strategy work done by UAE-headquartered firms with KSA operations; fabrication done by local KSA players

### Gaps Identified
- **Qatar**: Limited pure-wayfinding consultancies HQ'd in Qatar (mostly branch offices). Added: The Look Company (Doha), ELAN Urban (Doha), Gulfcrafts (fabrication), Pro Signs Qatar (digital).
- **Kuwait**: Limited local pure-play consultancies. Added: Sign Wood (full-service).
- **Bahrain**: Limited local specialists. Added: Awal Plastics (56-year regional leader).
- **Oman**: Limited local specialists. Added: ProSigns Oman, LMA Signs Oman, Oman Graphics Co LLC.
- **International firms with ME presence**: Brands like Minale Tattersfield, Minale Design Strategy have ME work but not dedicated regional presence
